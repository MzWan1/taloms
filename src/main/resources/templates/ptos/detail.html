Reinstate PTO PTO-2026-00001?

This will overturn the revocation and restore the PTO to ACTIVE status. The holder will regain all occupancy rights.

Only reinstate if the revocation was made in error or the issue has been resolved.
This reason will be recorded in the audit trail.

Revoke PTO PTO-2026-00001?

This action will permanently revoke the PTO. The holder will lose all occupancy rights. This cannot be undone.
